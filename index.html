Mbot2: Revolutionizing Coral Reef Protection

The Mbot2 is a cutting-edge robotic platform developed to aid in coral reef protection efforts. Designed for flexibility, precision, and efficiency, this advanced robot can perform a variety of tasks to help mitigate the challenges faced by marine ecosystems. Equipped with state-of-the-art sensors, AI-driven navigation systems, and a robust, eco-friendly design, Mbot2 is central to the Aquaboys' vision of cleaner and healthier oceans.

How Mbot2 Works

The Mbot2 operates both above and below the ocean surface, making it highly versatile for addressing coral reef threats. It is equipped with:

  • Waste Detection Sensors: Identifies and collects microplastics, fishing nets, and other debris around coral reef ecosystems.
  • Environmental Monitoring Tools: Measures water temperature, pH levels, and pollution concentrations to assess reef health.
  • Algae Removal Mechanism: Removes harmful algae overgrowth that blocks sunlight and disrupts coral growth.
  • AI-Assisted Navigation: Ensures safe and precise movements around delicate reef structures.
